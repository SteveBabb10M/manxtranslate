Bunneydys

Shey-Jeig: va - was, were

Nane: t'eh - it is

Jees: ta - is + weather

Tree: cha nel eh - he is not

Kiare: mee, oo - I, you

Queig: vel? is/are?

Shey: c'raad? - where?

Shiaght: 'sy - in the

Hoght: erbee - at all, any

Nuy: ec - at

Jeih: e - her

Nane-Jeig: quoi? who?

Daa-Yeig: er - on

Tree-Jeig: nagh vel? isn't?

Kiare-Jeig: ee - she, it

Queig-Jeig: vel eh? is he?

Shey-Jeig: va - was, were

Shiaght-Jeig: cha row - was not, were not

Hoght-Jeig: ayns - in

Nuy-Jeig: ayn - there, in

Feed: clag - clock (time)

Nane as Feed: quoid? how much?

Jees as Feed: nee eh...? is it a...?

Tree as Feed: stiagh, magh - in,out + past

Kiare as Feed: cha - not + past

Queig as Feed: cre'n traa? what time?

Shey as Feed: did he...?

Shiaght as Feed: lessons

Hoght as Feed: cha (negative)

Nuy as Feed: questions in the past...

Jeih as Feed - er e hon (for)

Nane-Jeig as Feed: hie as jagh (goll = to go)
