Tree: cha nel eh - he is not

Kiare: mee, oo - I, you

Queig: vel? is/are?

Shey: c'raad? - where?

Shiaght: 'sy - in the

Hoght: erbee - at all, any

Nuy: ec - at

Jeih: e - her

Nane-Jeig: quoi? who?
