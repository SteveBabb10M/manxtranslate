Notes on spelling preferences and variation in the Manx Bible
Max W. Wheeler
January 2019
The Bible is by far our most extensive repository of ‘standard’ Manx, of which orthography is a part. Some recent
work on proof-reading the Bible text (and Apocryphal books) for Yn Vible Ghaelgagh online
https://manx.global.bible/ has brought into focus exactly what this standard consists of. Though in a good number
of cases there is variation in spelling, often this variation displays a strong preference. I record below as preferred
spellings those which dominate over alternatives in a proportion of 2:1 or more.
After that I list most of the words where the alternatives are more evenly matched.
