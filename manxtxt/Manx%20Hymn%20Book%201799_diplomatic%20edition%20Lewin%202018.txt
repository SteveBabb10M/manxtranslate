GYS YN FER TA LHAIH.
AYNS ennaghtyn jeh feme lheid ny persoonyn as nagh row
toiggal Baarle; cha nee foddey er dy henney neayr’s va Lioar
Hymn printit ayns Gailck lesh ny smoo dy Hymnyn ayns y ghlare
cheddin, ny va er ny akin ayns print roish shen, er ny chiartaghey
cour ymmyd sleih-crauee jeh dy chooilley stayd choud’s va
mooads yn lioar fordrail eh, goit son yn chooid smoo voish
Hymnyn Wesley as Watts; ga shegin goaill rish, ayns scrieu as
ec printal yn Lioar cheddin, nagh row lheid yn chiarail goit as
lhisagh ve, hug oyr dy laane missalyn, as caghlaaghyn dy reddyn
lieh-chiart dy ve aynjee.
As myr t’an Lioar cheddin er dy henney er veeteil rish
lheid yn soiaghey ve jeant jee, liorish ymmoddee ayns yn Ellan
shoh, (er lheh nyn lheid as ta aignagh dy jinnagh tushtey jeh JEE
skeayley as bishaghey nyn mast’ain, as ta streeu dy chur yn foays
oc hene as feallagh elley er y hoshiaght,) er y choontey shoh ta ny
Hymnyn cheddyn nish er naase goun, myr shen ec y traa t’ayn,
nagh vod nyn lheid as ta laccal ny Lioaryn cheddin, ad y gheddyn.
[4]
Dy lhieeney seose er-y-fa shen ny ta jeeaghin dy ve foast
ergooyl, as feme nyn lheid as nagh vel ny Lioaryn shoh oc; t’an
nah lioar shoh er jeet magh, aynjee t’an caghlaa shoh veih [sic:
veih’n] chied lioar.
Hoshiaght,—T’an lioar goaill stiagh er gerrey da nuy-feed
dy Hymnyn, nyn mast oc ta jeih as da-eed dy Hymnyn noa nagh
row rieau ayns print roie ’sy ghlare ainyn, marish ny Hymnyn
vayns y chenn lioar, er-lhimmney [sic: er-lhimmey] jeh shiartanse
fardalagh jeu t’er ny aagail assdoo, nagh row ymmyd er ny
yannoo jeu agh an-vennick.
Ayns yn nah ynnyd,—Ta ny smoo dy chiarail er ny ghoaill
dy scrieu ny Hymnyn shoh, lurg yn aght ’scadjin ta’n Ghailck er
ny ve scruit ayn, as myrgeddin dy hoiaghey ny Hymnyn ayns lheid
yn aght, as dy bee ny Hymnyn jeh’n un sorch cooidjagh ayns y
lioar
Erjerrey.—Myr ta’n lioar er jeet magh lesh print share as
coontey smoo dy Hymnyn na’n lioar roie, ta shin jerkal dy jean ee
meeteil rish foayr ooilley nyn lheid as ta wooishal aigh vie da
cooish YEE.
[5]
HYMN I.
Cuirrey Peccee dy hyndaa gys Jee.
HO! tar-jee dagh unnane ta paa,
She Jee ta cuirrey tar-jee reue,
“Kionnee-jee bainney’s mill” t’eh gra,
Grayseyn yn sushtal nastee diu.
Tar-jee gys ushtey gell y vea,
Pheccee, she nyn Ver-croo ta geam;
Tar shiuish ta skee, as yiow shiu fea,
Voghtyn ta gennaghtyn nyn veme.
Jeeagh shiu veih Creest farrane ta gell!
Ayns strooanyn roie dy gherjagh shiu;
Tar shiuish ta laadit, skee, cha vel
Feme airh ny argid, y chur lhieu.
Kys ta shiu lheid ny reddyn reih,
Nagh vod diu maynrys cooie fordrail?
She bleaystyn follym ta shiu teiy.
Yn lab’ragh eu, ta ayns fardail.
Shirrey son maynrys noon as noal,
As gyn dy bragh dy gheddyn eh:
Cre’n raad O pheccee, bailliu goll?
Son ayms ta goan gerjoil y vea.
Eaishtee d’imneagh rish shen ta mie,
As ee-jee beaghey follan cooie, [6]
Prow-jee my vieys, as my ghraih,
Dy vel mee myghinagh as dooie.
Croym-jee dy arryltagh nyn gleaysh,
Cred-jee ny goan ta mee dy ghra;
Trooid credjue bee shiu troggit seose,
Gys bea dy vaynrys son dy bra.
HYMN II.
SHE lhean as feayn ta’n raad gys baase,
Thousaneyn annym er ta goll;
Agh er yn chassan coon dy ghrayse,
Cha vel agh troailtagh, noon as noal.
Gobbal shin hene, ’s goaill seose nyn grosh,
Shoh myr ta sarey’n Chiarn dy ghra:
Shegin da treigeil, dagh nhee ta wass,
Ta kiarit cosney’n vaynrys bra.
Yn annym agglagh ta gaase skee,
Gobbal dy hooyl yn raad gys niau;
T’eh coyrt e chooyl er raaidyn Yee,
As jannoo shickyr jeh toyrt-mow.
Hiarn, gow my pheccaghyn ersooyl,
As leeid mee ayns y chassan cair,
Raad nagh vod crauee oalsey shooyl,
Nagh vod mee-viallee goll er.
HYMN III.
TA Creest er n’aarlagh shibber vooar,
Lesh beaghey flaunyssagh dy-liooar,
As cour dagh annym t’echey room:
T’eh geam da ooilley tar-jee hym.
[7]
Ta Creest cur lesh e laanid hiu,
Cuirraghyn berchagh t’echey diu,
Nastee, ta Yeesey cur dagh nhee,
Pardoon, as marish glennid cree.
Nagh jean shiu leshtal ta mee guee,
Nagh soie shiu beg jeh cuirrey Yee;
Treig shiu dagh taitnys boght neu feeu;
Dy ghoaill yn ghrayse t’ec Yeesey diu.
Kys uirragh shiu veih lheid yn boayl,
Veih slaynt, as niart, as bea gherjoil!
Veih gloyr, as maynrys fegooish kione,
Veih sheshaght Chreest, as ooilley chloan.
Tar shiu ta laadit lesh angaish,
Ta shooyl gyn gerjagh shirrey aash;
O tar shiu voghtyn doal, as treih,
As cha jean Creest shiu yooildey [sic: yiooldey] veih.
My Hiarn graysoil, cha naillish coayl,
Yn peccagh s’treih, yn annym s’moal;
O tar shiu smessey as speccoil,
As yiow shiu Yeesey feer ghraysoil.
She nish ta’n earish jiu yn laa,
Eer nish t’eh geam diu dy hyndaa;
O tar shiu reue fegooish cumrail,
As Creest hur baase nee shiu hauail.
HYMN IV.
O Son thousane dy hengaghyn
Dy chur ard-ghloyr da Creest:
Dy voylley eh er son e ghraih,
E ghrayse, e chionnaght-reesht.
[8]
My Vainshter, as my Haualtagh,
O cooin uss lesh my ghoo.
Ayns skeayley fud y theihll dty ghloyr,
As ooashley dt’ennym noo.
Ta dt’ennym, Yeesey, gerjagh shin,
Geiyrt trimshey veih nyn gree;
Cre’n eunys t’ayn da peccee voght,
T’oc sauchys ayn as shee.
T’eh brishey pooar yn peccah baaish,
Lhiggey’n pryssoonagh free:
E uill nee glenney’n peccagh s’broie,
Son te er ghlenney mee.
Lhig da ny beuyr nish clashtyn rish,
Yn balloo ’voylley eh;
Lhig da ny doaill eh ’chur-my-ner,
Son yiow ad slane livrey.
Jeeagh-jee, ta slane nyn loght er Creest,
Ghow Eayn meen Yee ayns laue,
Dy chur son oural ’annym hene,
Son peccah slane sheelnaue.
O dooisht-jee ass nyn gadley broghe,
As Creest ver soilshey diu;
Cur-jee-nyn [sic: Cur-jee nyn] loght as sayntyn veue,
As niee-jee’n dooinney-doo:
Eisht marym’s s’broie va bee fys eu
Dy vel eu slane pardoon;
Feer vaynrey vees shiu ayns e ghraih,
Bee flaunys eu tra t’ayn
[9]
HYMN V.
Er Aalid craueeaght.
YN seihll s’fardalagh dagh nhee t’ayn,
Ga jeeaghin aalin dooin;
Son ayns e haitnys ta pyshoon,
Ta ’viljid cur lesh guinn.
Yn red smoo aalid fo yn aer,
Cre te agh caslys treih,
As ta shin shassoo ayns dangeyr,
Tra yoghe shin gerjagh veih.
Nyn moggey smoo, nyn gaarjyn s’deyr,
Cre’n aght t’ad goaill shin jeh;
T’ad goaill yn aigney harrish cair,
Dy s’coan yiow Jee agh lieh.
Graih gys ny reddyn t’er yn ooir,
Nagh trome te orrin lhie,
Geid lesh yn cree ’s te ass nyn booar,
Dy hayrn lesh reesht eh thie.
Haualtagh lhig da dt’aalid eisht,
Ve’n beaghey aym dagh oor,
As grayse, my chree dy slane chur lesh,
Veih reddyn er yn ooir.
HYMN VI.
BANNIT t’an dooinney, ta treigeil
Raaidyn yn sleih peccoil;
Raad ta drogh-yantee coyrt meeteil;
As ynnyd ny craidoil.
[10]
Agh goo yn Chiarn ta taitnys da,
Lane gerjagh t’echey veih;
T’eh lhaih ny geaishtagh rish ’sy laa,
As smooinaght er ’syn oie.
T’eh myr yn billey mie shen, ta
Rish oirr ny hawin messoil;
Sauchey veih’n ghea[y] ta fioghey’s scaah,
Ny hoie ayns boayl sheeoil.
Cha vel eh neu-vessoil as lhome;
Agh cronnal ta e vess;
Messyn graysoil, myr dhossyn trome,
T’er biljyn feeyney gaase.
Agh sleih peccoil cha vel myr shen,
Ta geiyrt da raad neu-chooie:
T’ad myr y coau ta lesh scaa-lhean
Eebyrit jeh’n laare-vooie.
Ayns briwnys cha vod peccee eisht
Shassoo fud deiney mie;
Tra nee yn briw Creest, coyrt lesh
Gys gloyr, ny nooghyn thie.
Yeesey dy voddym freayll dy leigh,
Niartee mee lesh dy ghrayse;
Dy voddym shaghney cronney’n sleih,
Ta shooyl yn raad gys baase.
HYMN VII.
TAR shiuish t’ayns graih rish Creest,
Lesh gennallys gherjoil,
Dy ghoail arraneyn moyllee nish,
Mygeayrt y mish e stoyl.
[11]
Adsyn nagh nhione daue Jee,
T’ad gobbal rooin goaill ayrn:
Agh foddee cloan yn niartal Ree
Dy kinjagh moylley’n Chiarn.
Yn Jee, ta er nyn skyn,
Ta fakin dagh ashoon;
T’eh markiaght er ny bodjallyn,
As jannoo’n faarkey kiune.
Shoh’n Jee dy atchim mooar,
T’eh dooin dagh nhee ta mie;
Ver eh neose chaghteryn e phooar,
Dy ghoaill shin huggey thie.
Eisht, hee mayd eh hur baase,
Gyn arragh veih chyndaa;
As eisht, veih awinyn jeh e ghrayse,
Giu maynrys son dy bra.
Agh roish my rosh mayd thie
Gys gloyr, ta gyn caghlaa:
Lhig smooinaghtyn er stayd cha mie,
Ve gerjagh dooin dagh traa.
Deiney t’er gheddyn grayse,
T’oc toshiaght gloyr ayns shoh:
Son messyn flaunyssagh, ta gaase,
Veih treisht as credjue bio.
Eisht lhig dooin moylley Jee,
As jeir ve currit voin:
Ta boggey Yeesey ayns nyn gree,
Gys seihll dy ghloyr goll roin.
[12]
HYMN VIII.
TA’N dooinney maynrey t’echey ayrn,
Ayns bannaght pobble reiht y Chiarn:
Yn chreenaght shen ta cheet veih Jee,
Credjue as graih ta ayns y chree.
Ta’n dooinney maynrey er skyn insh,
Echey ta credjue bio ayns Creest;
Er gheddyn gioot feer eunyssagh,
Er chosney tushtey flaunyssagh.
Creenaght veih niau, quoi oddys gra
Cre’n cosney yindyssagh t’ee da?
Roish argid ta shin soiagh’ee,
As airh cha vel feeu soylagh’ree.
Ta ’laueyn lane lesh liurid traa,
Berchys, as ooashley son dy bra,
Berchys veih Creest gyn eeck erbee,
Yn ooashley shen ta cheet veih Jee.
T’ee cuirrey shin dy ve gerjoil,
Lesh taitnys crauee spyrrydoil,
Ny raaidyn eck gerjoil da’n chree,
As slane e kesmadyn ta shee.
HYMN IX.
CHA vaik rieau sooill, cha geayll rieau, cleaysh,
Cha ghient eh rieau ’sy chree,
Dy hoiggal cre ta kiarit daue
Ta graihagh er Mac Yee!
[13]
Yn Ayr eh hene t’er chiarail eh,
As bee eh maynrys vooar;
Cur coontey jeh cha vod unnane,
Te lane erskyn nyn booar.
Spyrryd y Chiarn t’er hoilshagh ayrn,
Bee flaunyys [sic: flaunys] oc lurg baase:
As gloyr e ghoo ta daue londeyr,
Dy vod ad aynsyn gaase.
Feer vooar ta’n eunys ta ayns niau,
Ta ooilley boggey’s shee;
Agh eh ta tannaghtyn ayns olk,
Dy bragh ayns shen cha bee.
Ta giattyn niau jeight seose noi loght,
(Yiow peccee deyrey veayn)
Dy beagh unnane cha jig gys niau
Agh eiyrtyssee da’n Eayn.
Ta lioar y vea livreit gys Creest,
Ayn ta dagh ennym noo;
Nee crauee-oalsey ayns fardail
Dy gheddyn stiagh ayn streeu.
HYMN X.
MY Yee, uss bun my voggey-smoo,
She oo my vaynrys bra;
She oo my gherjagh ayns yn oie,
She oo my ghloyr ’sy laa.
Cha beagh yn oie shen dorragh’ dooys,
Ayn ennin uss my Yee; [14]
Veagh oo da m’annym myr rollage,
Myr grian dy hiow my chree.
Veagh niau myr fosley dy ghoaill rhym,
Cur skell jeh’n vaynrys veen;
Choud as veagh Yeesey soilshagh’ dou
Myr sonnish, “Tou lhiam pene,”
O s’gennal scarrin rish yn ooir
Tra ennin lheid y shee
Etlin lesh boggey seose gys niau
Gys roiaghyn Creest my Yee.
Cha beign er-creau roish baase ny jouyl,
Chamoo rish [sic: roish] nhee dy beagh;
Trooid credjue Chreest, as ayns e ghraih,
Veagh aym slane barriaght.
HYMN XI.
Er Mieys Yee.
JEEAGH kys t’eh treinit cass as laue,
Saualtagh seihll peccoil;
Nagh mooar va’ ghraih, dy daag eh niau,
Son ain dy gheayrtey uill!
Clasht rish e accan! jeeagh cre’n craa,
T’er ghreimmey’n ooir veih heese!
Coodagh yn chiamble raipit ta,
Ny creggyn creoi ta brisht.
Te jeant yn lhiasagh er my hon,
Gow m’annym eisht t’eh guee!
Jeeagh, kys t’eh crommey sheese e chione,
Chroym eh, as phaart Mac Yee!
[15]
Agh s’leah t’eh cur yn baase ass bree,
Soilshean ayns gloyr as pooar:
O quoi hur pian myr ren Eayn Yee?
Quoi ayns va graih cha mooar?
HYMN XII.
TREINIT gys billey’r Calvary,
Coodit lesh ollish as lesh fuill;
O jeeagh er Ree dy chooilley Ree!
Crommey gys baase ta’n Jee graysoil.
Quoi Hiarn, t’er n’yannoo’n obbyr shen?
Quoi ren dty chorp gloyroil y vroo?
Dty chree veih loght dy kinjagh glen,
Chamoo va foalsaght ayns dty ghoo.
She mish, eer mish t’er nirree dt’oi,
Hug oo gys seaghyn t’erskyn goan,
Hug er dty uill smoo deyr dy roie,
Yn chrown dy ghrine chur er dty chione
Yn Ree dy ghloyr cre vel eh nish?
Yn Mac gloyroil jeh lheid yn stayd,
E oaie lesh trimshey croghey sheese,
As neesht gannooinagh fo yn laad.
She fo my laad hur oo yn baase,
Mish ren dty choyrt gys wheesh dy gheir,
Ren cur ort geam lesh wheesh d’angaish,
As marroo Mac smoo deyr yn Ayr.
Eayn Yee smoo graihagh as gloyroil,
S’graihagh er peccee ta dty chree;
Cooin lhiam dy ennaght bree dty uill,
Dty ghraih ayns baase ren kionnaght mee.
She mish va treigit, boght, as faase,
My lhie ayns beeal yn lion dewil,
Uss, lheim ayns cabbyn geyr y vaaish,
Dy my livrey veih pooar yn jouyl.
Cur dooys dy ennaghtyn dty phian,
Goaill bine jeh’n chappan sharroo neesht,
Gymmyrkey’n chrosh mayrt hene yn Eayn,
As ayrn ve aym ayns surranse Chreest.
Haualtagh graihagh cooin uss lhiam,
Yn currym t’orrym y choilleen,
Dy vod dy chooilley nhee ta aym,
Ve baarit gys yn ghloyr ayd hene.
Still lhig dty osnaghyn as jeir,
Kinjagh cur er my hooillyn roie;
Derrey veem troggit seose ’syn aer,
As mayrt ayns yrjey gloyr ny hoie!

V’ayns mac yn niartal Jee,

Row chymmey rieau myr chymmey Chreest
Son s’mie va echey fys,

[17]
Nyn beccaghyn she shen va’n oyr
Hur Yeesey Creest y pian;

Nish ga t’eh reill ec laue yesh Yee,
Cha vel e ghraih veg sloo;
T’eh cooinaght foast er Calvary
Vs [sic: As] er e gheiney noo.
HYMN XIV.
O Ghaih-flaunys, cre ta jeant ayd!
She Jee t’er hurranse baase son aym
Mac corrym rish yn Ayr ayns stayd
T’er n’ymmyrkey my pheccaghyn!
Son aym ta Jee er hurranse baase,
My Hiarn, my ghraih t’eh eshyn cross’t.
O gow-jee yndys [sic: yindys] shiuish pheccee
Jeh surranse Chreest, eh Prince y vea:
Tar-jee, son eu roie fuill e chree,
O row rieau seaghyn myr hur eh!
Tar ennee marym bree e vaase,
My Hiarn, my ghraih t’eh eshyn cross’t.
T’eh eshyn cross’t son aym as eu,
Dy hayrn shin shaghrynee gys Jee:
Cred, cred, ta’n feanish er dy rieau,
Ta shin kionnit ec fuill mac Yee:
Pardoon as bea ta ayns e vaase,
My Hiarn, my ghraih t’eh eshyn cross’t.
Nish lhig dooin soie ec bun e chrosh,
As giu jeh ushtey gell y vea;
Dagh nhee hreigeil er coontey Chreest,
As cur nyn greeaghyn seose huggey;
Nyn ghoan nyn smooinaght lhig daue ve
My Hiarn, my ghraih t’er ny chrossey.
HYMN XV.
Er Baase.
YNSEE dou towse my laghyn giare,
Uss my Er-croo graysoil;
Baillym goaill tastey jeu ny share,
Cha siyragh as t’ad goll.
She lhiurid reish ta er nyn son,
Cha giare shen ta nyn draa;
Ta dooinney eisht fardail as joan,
Ayns ’aalid as y vlaa.
Jeeagh er sheelnaue, son red neu-feeu,
’Sy choan shoh wass rouail;
Tooilleil as boirey, geam as streeu,
As t’ooilley’n sheean fardail.
Paart geearree ooashley seihltagh ta,
Paart elley berchys reih,
Cour eriaghyn gyn fys quoi da,
Ad hene dy leah goit veih.
Cre er neem’s soiagh eisht my imnea,
Yn seihll, ny cooney sleih?
Yn jerkal ain t’ad giarey jeh,
As molley shin dy treih.
’Sy theihll cha jeanym cur my hreisht
Ny soiagh’ er, my chree; [19]
Agh cur my yerkal seihltagh seose,
As credjal ayns my Yee.
HYMN XVI.
KYS veagh shin keayney choud dy hraa,
Ec baase persoonyn mie:
Nagh nee nyn Jiarn t’ayn coyrt coraa,
Dy eam ad huggey thie.
Nagh vel shin huggey neesht scapail,
Cha chion as traa ta roie;
Chamoo t’ain yeearree dy chumrail,
Ny sodjey voish nyn n’ghraih.
Kys ghoghe shin aggle dy chur sheese,
Nyn girp ’syn oaye gys fea?
Raad lhie corp oasle Yeesey Creest,
’S t’er yannoo manyrey jeh.
Ta bannaght Yee er deiney mie,
Nyn gadley ayns y joan:
Cre beagh ny oltyn booiagh lhie
Ayns baase, agh raad va’n kione?
Agh ta nyn Jiarn er n’irree reesht,
Ginsh dooin yn raad gys gloyr,
Nee’n eill ain getlagh seose gys Creest
Ec laa yn irree vooar.
HYMN XVII.
TRA irreem’s veih yn lhiabbee vaaish
Laadit lesh atchim mooar
Sumnit kion-fenish my Er-croo
kys nee’m eisht gansoor.
[20]
Nish choud’s ta myghin, as pardoon,
Daue t’er y hon chyndaa!
Dy vel my annym eer er-creau,
Ec smooinaght er y traa!
Tra ne’oo, O Hiarn, gyn coodagh cheet,
Ayns ooashley ard as pooar,
Er m’annyms soie ayns briwnys gyere,
O kys neem’s eisht gansoor?
O lhig dou lesh cree imlee brisht,
Keayney my pheccah traa,
Moghey ’ve arryssagh ayns jeir
Scapail yn treihys vra.
Roish te ro-anmagh, cur-my-ner
My hrimsey laa as oie;
As eaisht rish accan baaish my Hiarn,
Dy chur da’n trimmid cooie.
Cha beem’s ayns dooyt, dy gheddyn nish
Pardoon, son loght my vea,
Fys t’aym dy hur dty vac hene baase,
Dy yannoo shickyr jeh.
HYMN XVIII.
SHE’N vea shoh’n traa dy hirveish Jee,
Dy yannoo shickyr bea, as shee;
Choud as ta’n sushtal geamagh da,
Foddee yn peccagh smoo chyndaa.
Yn traa ta Jee dooin’ er charail,
Baase as coayl-anmey dy scapail:

Agh s’leah ta nyn stayd er chaghlaa,
Ta’n spyrryd er etlagh er-sooyl!
Ta’n oie ain, ayns ynnyd yn laa,
As nish lesh angaish ta shin shooyl.
Cre nee mayd dy chosney dty ghrayse,
Yn ghraih va ain keayrt ayns nyn gree?
Ny baase veagh y [sic: eh] dooin geddyn baase,
Ny beaghey gys ainjys er Jee!
Lesh seaghyn dy kinjagh dooyteil,
Lesh atchim, angaish, as imnea.
Mygeayrt yn ard-valley tooilleil,
Er-derrey ver Yeesey dooin fea.
Shiuish arreyderyn diuish ver mayd raaue,
My nee shiu yn graih ain veeteil,
(Ta foddey s’gloyroil ny sheelnaue!)
As shi’yn gys nyn garrey leeideil,
Dy voddagh shin fakin e oaie,
Dy scughey nyn seaghyn ta wheesh;
Son da ta shin booiagh cur graih,
O cooin nee mayd geddyn eh reesht?

HYMN LI.

Er-gerrey dhyt dy hayrn,
Goaill-rish dy vel oo firrinagh:
As craa ec goo yn Chiarn.

Ta cur er jeir dy roie;
Nagh jean ny sodjey shassoo magh,
Noi ayds, ta abyl stroie.

Jean gobbragh’ ayns my chree;

As dy voym baase ayns shee.
My jean y laa dy heaghyn cheet,
Ne’oo mish y scughey veih.

HYMN LII.
O Cooinee orrym uss hur baase,
Cha vod oo hene dy bragh jarrood
Dty ollish foalley as angaish,
Ny pianyn sharroo, hie-oo trooid.
Dy jeean ayns padjer laboragh,
Yn spyrryd laadit coayl e vree:
Son feill cha voddagh shassoo magh
Roish corree’n ooilley-niartal Ree.
My Ayr, my ta mee nish lhiat hene,
Cur clashtyn da my accan faase: [59]
As gow yn laad dy pheccah jeem;
Nagh beem fo deyrey geddyn baase.
She shoh ta oyr lane aggle dooys,
As laadey sheese my annym treih,
Aggle dy sur-ym’s dty yimmoose,
Choud as vees eashyn beayn dy roie.
Ayns padjer ta mee hood chyndaa,
Aggle y vaaish gow slane er-sooyl,
Baase ta cheet baggyrt lesh e ghah,
As niurin agglagh cheet ny chooyl.
Agh Hiarn nagh, [sic] jean uss m’y aagail,
Dy huittym void fo deyrey beayn:
Agh trooid dty vac jean m’y hauail,
Quoi er my hon hur wheesh dy phian.
HYMN LIII.
O Te dy liooar! my Yee ghraysoil,
Nagh lhig dou skirraghtyn ny smoo;
Gyn arragh stampey er dty uill,
As gyn ny sodjey brasnagh’oo,
As gyn ny sodjey girree magh,
Noi graih, as soilshey flaunyssagh.
My t’ou dy jarroo slane chymmoil,
Hiarn soilshee nish dty graih as shee;
Eer dooys jeh peccee’n fer s’peccoil,
Ta imlee son dty vyghin guee;
Gys foar yn Ayr jean m’y chaghlaa,
Gyn arragh brasnagh’oo dy bra.
Farrane dy ghraih nagh jean goll mow,
My accan faase jean soiagh’ jeh; [60]
My charrey dooie, ta heose ayns niau,
O nish! jean shassoo ass my leh;
Cur arrys, credjue, as pardoon,
As jean mee slane lhiat hene traa t’ayn.
HYMN LIV.

Erskyn dty ghrayse as dty erreeish;
Da peccee voghtey t’ou chymmoil,

O niee my annym veih dagh broid
Jean my choinsheanse y glenney royd
Ta errey m’oiljyn guin my chree,
Ta’n chooinaght oc goaill voym my hee.
My veeal lesh nearey ta goaill roo,
T’ad noi dty ghrayse, t’ad noi dty ghoo:
Dy bailt ’chur orrym briwnys geyre,
Veagh deyrey cair dou, t’ou uss cleer.
Ga mennick er dty vrasnagh Hiarn,
Nagh jean dty spyrryd voym y hayrn,
Agh orryms soilshee dty erreeish,
As cur dou ayrn ayns toilchin Chreest.
Nee’m gynsagh eisht dty raad da’n sleih,
Nee peccee chionney gys dty ghraih;
Nee’m ad leeideil gys fuill y chee,
Yiow moylley son dty ghrayse, my Yee.
[61]

Agh veih dagh peccah scuir.

Tar Hiarn, as gow mee stiagh gys foayr,
Ansoor my accan jeean,

Goaill ayrn jeh’n dooghys flaunyssagh,

Jean cur er-sooyl dagh nhee neu-ghlen.

YEESEY, cur geill veih niau
Da accan dty chretoor;

Trog me erskyn yn ooir,
Veih peccah jean mee seyr,

Dty uill as cairys, ta

Ta voids dy kinjagh gell,
Awinyn jeh ushtey’n vea,
[69] Roie voish dty vieys dou;

T’oc ayrn jeh boggey niau.
Tar, soilshee er cheu-sthie,
Dty chairys, ayns my chree;
Dy voddym blashtyn cre cha mie,
Cha cha graysoil’s ta Jee;
Dy voddym soilshagh magh,
Yn phooar ayd dy hauail:
Seyr liorish credjue gimmeeaght:
’Sy chredjue shen paartail.
HYMN LXV.
YEESEY, my charrey son dy bragh,

Cooin lhiam dy streeu dy jeean;
Lesh daanys crauee lhieen my chree,
Lesh credjue dy ghoaill greme er Jee,
Uss ta my chronney beayn.

As da my yeir chymmoil;

Ta nish lesh lheid y jeeanid guee,
My phadjer slane breeoil.
Cur dooys dty ghrayse dty ghraih cur dou,
Dty spyrryd gaccan er my cheu,
As shen t’ou cur-my-ner.
T’eh cooney lesh m’annooinid vooar, [70]
As gaccan er my hon lesh pooar,
Ta foddey erskyn glare.
Hiarn jean dty spyrryd y chooilleen,
As soilshee dou dty ghooghys hene,
Dty ennym graihagh Yee:
Myr ta dty ennym shen myr t’ou,
Soilshee yn dooghys mie ayd dou,
As scrieu eh er my chree.

Yn Chiarn jeh sheshaght-chaggee niau,
Yn Chiarn smoo graihagh oo:
Dy vyghin vooar cha jean failleil,
Yn Jee nagh vel dy bragh treigeil

Goaill arrys, bea y reih:

Yn shaghrynagh chyndaa:
T’eh booiagh goaill dagh annym nish,
Tar Hiarn dy bieau as chaggle mish,
Tar my haualtagh bra.

Dy gheddyn soylley slane my hreisht,
Abbyr rhym goll dy siyragh seose,
Er dt’oghrish paartail.

CLASHT rish my eam, my Yee, my Yee
Ort baillym nish cur enn;
Ayns fuill yn Eayn jean mish y niee,
Dy voddym nish ’ve glen.

HYMN LXVIII.
SKEE jeh rouail choud voids my Yee,

Keayney son ayd, agh ayns treishteil;

As niee my annym ayns dty uill,

HYMN LXIX.

Cuin vees my chree ta arryltagh [75]
Goit seose lesh shen ny t’ou?

Dy hoiggal mooads dty ghraih ayns baase,
Yn ghraih hug Yeesey dou!
E ghraih ny stroshey ta ny’n baase;
Diunid dy verchys nagh vel faase
Ec ny Ard-ainlyn beayn,
Dy ronsagh folliaght ta wheesh;
Lhiurid, as lhead, as diunid neesht,

HYMN LXXXVII.
BOOISE as gloyr da Jee yn Ayr,
Jee, e ghloyr ta lhieeney’n aer,
Gys cloan gheiney dy row shee,
Aigney mie, as foayr veih Jee.
Streeu dy voylley lesh un chree
Uss; nyn Ayr gloyroil, Ree:
Jee dy phooar as graih, dy bragh
Nagh vod shin y ronsagh’ magh.
[94]
Ooilley’n croo, nish lhig daue streeu,
Dy chur gloyr dhyt t’er dy rieau;
Shi’yn dy booisal, oddys gra,
Jee dy ghraih, fegooish caghlaa.
Yeesey, t’ou dooin Jee as Chiarn,
Mac! yn ayr dy bragh er-mayrn,
Creest ren surranse er y chrosh,
Son ain peccee ayns shoh wass.
Uss, ta loayrt son ain rish Jee,
Jean shin ayns dty uill y niee;
Cur ansoor as tar dy chion,
Uss yn lhiasagh er nyn son.
Eaisht, O Chreest, ayns niau soilshean,
Marish dt’ayrn gloyroil unnane:
Mayrt yn Spyrryd-Noo dty lheid,
Gloyr, as moylley, da’n Trinaid.
HYMN LXXXVIII.
ER-sooyl voym aggle as dooyteil:
Cha beem’s ny smoo ayns stayd cha treih
Ga my Haualtragh foast cha vel
Er hoilshagh aalid gloyr e oaie:
Agh nee son shen neem’s lhiggey da
Yn raad, as lhiantyn da’n volteyr?
Cha jean, trooid niart y Chiarn dy bra,
Nee’m streeu as cur my varrant er.
Ga ragh yn billey-feeney mow,
Yn billey-olive fegooish ooil,
Ny biljyn-figgagh fioghey roue,
As mess yn vaghyr scaht er-sooyl; [95]
Ga nagh beagh veg jeh’n maase er-mayrn,
As murran gastyrt yn chioltane;
Ny yeih nee’m gloyraghey ’sy Chiarn;
Gys my Haualtagh goym arrane.
Ga ta my annym’s tannaghtyn
Ayns stayd cha chyrym, neu-vessoil,
Follym jeh grayse as bannaghtyn;
Gyn veg y vie, agh slane peccoil;
Ga ta my gherjagh tilgit sheese,
My yerkal follym giarit jeh;
Ny-yeih nee’m boggey ghoaill ayns Creest,
T’er yannoo lhisagh [sic: lhiasagh] ass my lieh.
HYMN LXXXIX.
TA farrane ayn ta lhieent lesh fuill
Ren roie veih Eayn y chee;
Dy voddagh peccee veih dagh foill,
Ayns shen ve er ny niee!
Yn maarliagh hene ghow boggey mooar,
Tra ren eh fakin eh:
As mish va neesht neu-ghlen dy liooar,
T’ayns shen er my livrey!
Fuill Chreest cha jean ee coayl y bree,
Agh kinagh pooaral ta;
Derrey vees ooilley nooghyn Yee
Goit seose gys maynrys bra.
Neayr ren mee fakin yn farrane,
Ta sauchys aym as aash;
Graih flaunyssagh t’er ve m’arrane,
As bee er gys my vaase.
[96]
Eisht ayns aght s’ooasle ta fys aym,
Nee’m singal jeh dty phooar;
Tra vees yn chengey voandagh t’aym,
Ny lhie ec fea ’syn ooir.
She er my hon dy shicker ta
Greie-chiaullee heose ayns niau;
Dy voylley Jee er son dy bra,
Shen gioot va kionnit dou.
T’eh jeant as shiaullit son aym neesht,
Dagh nhee ta ayns y chair
Dy voylley ennym Yeesey Creest,
Gys ooashley ard yn Ayr.
HYMN XC.
O tar-jee lhig dooin goaill arrane,
Myr ainlyn roish yn stoyl,
Thousane d’housaneyn myr unnaue, [sic: unnane],
Ta moylley eh lesh kiaull.
Feer feeu ta’n Eayn hur baase t’ad gra,
Dty [sic: dy] chosney ooashley beayn;
Feer feeu ta’n Eayn ta’n cree gimraa,
Son peccee hur eh’n pian.
Ta Yeesey feeu jeh ooashley vooar,
Jeh gloyr as pooar dy bra;
As moylley smoo ny t’ayns nyn booar,
Vees kinjagh currit da.
Lhig da’n slane croo nish myr unnane,
Moylley yn ennym beayn;
Gys Jee yn Ayr sheer goaill arrane!
As ooashley chur da’n Eayn
[97]
HYMN XCI.
LHIG da dy chooilley nhee cur booise
Da’n Chiarn, Jehovah nyn ver-croo,
Moyll shiu eh niau as thalloo neesht,
Ren shin ass veg lesh pooar e ghoo.
Tra ren eh loayrt yn goo breeoil,
Dagh nhee ren freggyrt e choraa;
Liorish e ghoo [e Vac] gloyroil,
Dagh nhee va toshiaght currit da.
Yeesey, yn Chiarn, as Jee mooar shen,
Ren cummey slane sheelnaue, as mish,
Mish, son dy voddin cur er enn’,
As eh dy biallagh ’hirveish.
As nish (my ver oo hene dou pooar,)
Dy arryltagh yiow slane my chree;
As my nee’m dt’aigney er yn ooir.
Dy leah vee’m mayrt ayns niau my Yee.
HYMN XCII.
TROG-jee nyn sooillyn seose gys niau,
Raad ta’n Saualtagh soie,
Ta ass nyn lieh loayrt rish e Ayr,
Ayns graih t’eh son eu guee.
Son ayds, O m’annym, hur eh’n baase,
As gheayrt eh fuill e chree;
Aggyrts y leigh ren eh chooilleen’,
Eisht hie eh seose gys Jee.
Padjer as booise vees soit jeu nish,
My t’ad veih grunt y chree; [98]
Nee Creest nyn saggyrt goaill nyn gooish
Dy hoiagh roish y Ree.
Nee Creest cur lesh my phadjeryn,
Kiongoyrt rish stoyll e Ayr;
Trooid toilchin Chreest bee soiagh jeant
Jeh m’osnaghyn as jeir.
O Ayr, jean leih daue, nee eh gra,
She son oc hur mee baase;
Cha baillym un unnane jeu ’choayl,
O cur daue foayr as grayse.
Jymmoose yn ayr ta leah chyndait
Trooid accan nyn bleadeyr;
Gra, Vac trooid grayse nee’m ad hauail,
As er dty ghraih’s t’ad seyr.
O cur-jee booise da’n Ayr graysoil,
As moyll-jee ’ennym noo;
Cur-Jee [sic: Cur-jee] dagh gloyr da Yeesey Creest,
As gloyr da’n Spyrryd Noo.
HYMN XCIII.
LHIG dooin goaill arrane,
Marish flaunyssee,
Dy voylley nyn Jiarn,
Lesh firrinys cree;
Shen t’ain nish ayns soylley,
Ta feeu jeh nyn mooise;
Dagh gloyr as dagh moylley,
Ta cairagh da Creest.
Ard-voylley gys Jee,
Ta reill er-nyn-skyn; [99]
T’er ghiootal dy free,
As er chur neose hooin
Creest Yeesey saualtys,
As gerjagh e chloan;
T’eh eearlys jeh maynrys,
As bea fegooish kione.
COOIE as cair te moylley Jee,
Dagh traa, as ayns dagh saase;
Gloyr y choyrt da Creest nyn Ree,
Yn Jee ta lane dy ghrayse:
Lhig dooin ooilley nish goaill ayrn,
As toyrt-booie y hebbal da:
Ooashley feeu y chur da’n Chiarn,
Eer nish, as son dy bra.
Ad ta heose ayns soilshey dt’oaie,
Ta nish lesh un choraa:
Moylley laa liauyr fegooish oie,
Nagh vel goaill fea dy bra;
Ainlyn as ard-ainlyn neesht.
Moylley’n Three-unnane gloyroil;
Cur dhyt ooashley, tuittym sheese,
Ayns yindyss roish dty stoyl.
Baillin cur dhyt moylley neesht,
Er son dty Vac hur baase;
Da nyn Jiarn cur moylley’s booise,
Ta dooin cha lane dy ghrayse;
As da Jee yn Spyrryd Noo,
Ammys feeu y hebbal da, [100]
Troggal kiaull lhig ooilley’n croo,
As ooir gys niau hyndaa,
HYMN XCV.
AYR flaunyssagh, she liort
T’ain bioys, niart as bree;
Dhyts baillin booise as moylley choyrt
Son croo shin, as dagh nhee:
Shiuish ainlyn cur shiu da
Ard ghloyr lesh slane ny booar;
As deiney’n chiaull gherjoil hyndaa,
Gys flaunys veih yn ooir.
Nish, as er son dy bra,
Da Jee yn Mac cur booise,
Shin hene myr oural ’hebbal da
Ren shin y Chionnaght reesht;
E ghraih lhig flaunyssee
Insh magh trooid eashyn beayn;
As geam saualtys ard gys Jee,
Saualtys gys yn Eayn.
Lhig da dagh noo cur booise
Da spyrryd bannit Yee;
Ta tayrn lesh shaghrynee gys Creest,
E phooar ta glenney’n cree!
Ainlyn cha vod dty ghraih
Ta wheesh, y hoilshagh magh;
Ny strooanyn maynrey void ta roie,
Dy voggey flaunyssagh.
Lhig sheshaght-chaggee niau
Cur gloyr da’n Three-unnane; [101]
Dhyts lhig da ooilley cloan sheelnaue
Cur graih, as goaill arrane:
Yn aer ’s yn ooir un laa
Nee chea kiongoyrt rish dt’oaieː
Agh eisht yiow moylley son dy bra,
Veih ooilley cloan dty ghraih.
HYMN XCVI.
MOYLL-jee yn Chiarn shiuish ainlyn niau,
Ta cur-my-ner e oaie:
Ren shiu jeh stoo nagh vel goll mow,
’Nyn meaghey lesh e ghraih.
Gow-jee arrane, son diuish te cair
Cur moylley dy creeoil;
As crommey er ny straidyn airh,
Roish Jee Ta cha gloyroil.
Yn Ghrian ta ’choorse dy kinjagh roie,
Dy chur dooin soilshey’n laa,
Lhig da goaill rish, (as eayst yn oie)
Quoi ta cur soilshey da.
Geayghyn, ansoor shiu da ’choraa,
Dy ynsagh shin ayns shoh:
Son tra ta’n bodjal fainagh da,
Ta shiuish myr queeylyn fo.
Lhig sterrym, dorrin, as dagh nee,
Eer, taarnagh as tendreil,
Lhig daue cheet roue, as atchim Yee
Da cloan sheelnaue phreacheil.
[102]
Yn faarkey soilshagh magh dty phooar,
Dy kinjagh lhieenney’s traih:
Lhig touyn gys touyn lesh booise gansoor,
Baaie freggyt neesht gys baaie.
As beishtyn ayns y diunid vooar,
Lesh scaailey sollys creoi,
Ad shoh ta soilshagh dooin dty phooar,
Myr t’ad yn faarkey spreih.
Agh reddyn elley foddey s’kiune
Gys dt’ennym ghoys arrane;
Yn chiaull oc bingys rish y throoan,
As roie fud dagh banglane.
Croym-jee lesh ooashley da’n fer-croo
Dy chooilley villey glass;
As dagh banglane ta laane dy hoo,
Gys Jee hug erriu gaase.
Ny heeanlee troggal seose veih’n ooir
Lesh kiaull, ec brishey’n laa:
As geeck nyn geesh lhig dagh cretoor,
Dy rere myr ta ’choraa.
Choud’s ta cretooryn troggal kiaull,
Lhig deiney moylley Jee:
As lesh un aigney ayns dagh boayl
Cur gloyr da’n Chiarn nyn Ree.
[103]
HYMN XCVII.
Prayll.
YEESEY t’ou Chiarn erskyn dagh nhee,
Er son dy bragh fegooish failleil:
Eaisht rish dy [sic: dty] phobble hood ta guee,
As O jean gynsagh ad dy phrayll;
Spyrryd dy accan jeean breeoil,
Deayrt orrin neose, O Ayr ghraysoil.
Cha vel ain eer yn vieys sloo,
Smooinaght ny yeearree mie erbee,
Gys nee uss ren yn seihll y chroo,
Yn phooar y hoilshagh ayns nyn gree;
Eisht ta shin ayns dty spyrryd geam,
As cur dhyt reesht dty ghiootyn hene.
Dty chloan ta hood ayns seaghyn geam;
Cur clashtyn da nyn badjeryn,
Cooilleen nyn yeearree, as nyn veme,
Ayns traa dangeyr, as miolaghyn;
Cordail rish firrinys dty ghoo,
Deayrt orrin neose yn Spyrryd Noo.
D’ymmyrkey lesh annooinnid vooar,
dty phobble hene ta brisht ayns cree;
Dy vod mayd gleck rhyt hene lesh pooar,
As barriaght v’ain marish Jee:
Spyrryd dy accan jeean cur hooin,
As jean dty enish hene chur dooin.
Spyrryd dy accan ass nyn leih
Cur dooin, ta er dty hon waiteil: [104]
Erskyn dagh gioot, cur dooin dy ve
Abyl dy chooilley hraa dy phrayll:
Yn bannaght shoh cur dooin O Yee!
As marish ver oo dooin dagh nhee.
HYMN XCVIII.
DTY Spyrryd deayrt er ooilley’n ooir,
Ayns cairys croo ee ass-y-noa:
Lhig da’n reeriaght ayd cheet lesh pooar,
As reeriaght niurin tuittym fo.
Myr thooilley niartal lhig e [sic: eh] cheet,
As roish dy chooilley noid y stroie:
Derrey vees ooilley’n seihll shoh reilt,
Lesh credjue gobbragh’ liorish graih.
Lhig da dty spyrryd ayns dagh boayl
Dy palchey skeayley magh e vree;
Derrey nee graih, as mess graysoil,
Blaaghey, as skeayley ayns dagh cree.
Shoh jean y yialdyn dooin, O Ayr,
Uss ta dy bragh dty ghoo cooilleen;
Cooilleen dty ghialdyn, dooin te cair,
Ayns aile dy ghraih soilshee oo hene.
HYMN XCIX.
O Spyrryd Noo, tar hooin,
Lesh gerjagh gys dagh cree:
Soilshee yn Ayr nish coardit rooin
Trooid fuill Mac graihagh Yee:
Tar cur dooin shee as aash.
As sooillyn d’akin Creest: [105]
Eh hur, son dagh drogh-yantagh baase,
Son aym’s nagh hur eh neesht?
’Nane cha vel abyl gra,
Yeesey, e Hiarn dy ve,
Derrey ne’oo coyrt y hoilshey da,
As ayrn ayns goo yn vea;
Eisht ta shin gennaght pooar
Yn uill ta cur dooin shee;
As geam lesh graih, as boggey mooar,
She oo my Hiarn, my Yee!
Dy goghe dagh annym rish
Hug er nyn son e uill;
Tar Spyrryd Noo, as soilshee nish,
E annym smoo breeoil;
(Yn ghrayse ta chebbit daue,
Yn phooar ta cheet veih Jee,)
As bee leeideilagh slane sheelnaue,
As reill dy chooilley chree.
Cur dooin yn credjue bio,
Eshyn ta cosney’n gioot,
Ta echey fenish jeh bea noa,
As credjal fegooish dooyt;
Dagh noid t’eh lhieggal sheese,
Ny sleityn t’eh dy lheie,
Sauail dagh ’nane ta geam er Creest;
Jeant firrinagh ayns graih.
HYMN C.
SHASS liorish dt’eirtyssee my Yee
Lesh pooar breeoil dty ghrayse; [106]
As cur daue yeearee jeean nyn gree,
Yn rass t’ad cuirr dy aase.
Foshil dagh cree dy ghoaill dty ghoo,
Choud’s ta dty yialdyn gra
Dy vel oo booiagh coardail roo,
My nee ad hood chyndaa.
Eisht lhig thousaneyn chyndaa hood,
Ec eam breeoil dty ghrayse;
Dy vod ad myghin cheddyn [sic: gheddyn] trooid,
Yn Eayn ren surranse baase.
My Yee, jeeagh neose er peccee voaght,
T’ayns dorraghys ny lhie;
Cur daue dy ennaghtyn nyn loght,
As grayse dy hyndaa thie.
Chaggle ny peccee veih dagh ayrn,
(Ga t’ad ayns coontey wheesh;)
Dy vod ad ve un woaillee Hiarn,
Fo’n bochill graihagh Creest.
HYMN CI.
YEESEY, eaisht rish eam dty noogyn, [sic: nooghyn,]
Ayns dty ennym hene ta prayll;
Nish ayns myghin croym dty niaughyn,
Da dty phobble cur meeteil:
Da nyn accan jeean cur clashtyn,
Cur dooin nish ansoor dy hee;
Lhig dooin er dty vieys blashtyn,
Tar lesh gerjagh gys dagh cree.
Jeeagh c’wooad sooill gys niau ta troggit,
Whilleen cree son Yeesey fieau,
[107]	Accan jeean nagh vod ve obbit,
Geamagh, “Yesey, tar dy bieau;”
Baillin jeh dty ghraih goaill soylley;
Jean dty noidyn ’hilgey sheese;
Gow yn reiltys as yn moylley,
Lhieen dagh cree lesh graih, as booise.
Fockle feer gherjoilagh chlinym,
M’annym booisal da’n coraa;
“Raad vees jees ny three ayns m’ennym,
Beem’s ayns shen,” ta Yeesey gra:
Nish my t’ou uss Hiarn er gerrey,
Lhig dooin gennaghtyn dty phooar:
O haualtagh! tar ny sniessey,
Soilshee dty haualtys vooar.
As dy bragh nagh jean treigeil shin,
Freill dy chooilley annym faase;
Trooid yn aasagh jean leeideil shin,
Bee nyn garrey dooie gys baase;
Eisht my Hiarn ’syn oor femoil shen,
Jean dty phobble y hauail:
Eer ec baase, bee feer ghraysoil dooin,
Lhig dooin ayns dty hee paartail.

HYMN CV.
JEE jeh my vea, dty phooar graysoil,
Trooid gaueyn baaish er my leeideil,
Er hyndaa voym yn oor baasoil,
Freayll seose my chione tra vein sinkeil.
Ayns slane my raad goym rish dty roih,
Dty ard chiarail hug lesh mee trooid,
Cooin lhiam ’sy raad kinjagh dy roie,
My chesmadyn leeid jeeragh hood.
Yn faarkey goaill rish pooar dty roih,
Er my livrey ec sarey Yee;
Cha voddagh eh my vioys ’stroie,
Son uss my Hiarn, va coadey mee.
Er broogh yn oaye ren oo sparail,
Cha ren oo freayll dty chooney voym,
Hooar mee oo faggys dy hauail,
Dy eiyrt er-sooyl yn chingys trome.
Quoi gys nee’m goll son veg y vie?
Agh chea gys my haualtagh beayn;
Sauchey ayns dt’oghrish dy lhie,
As fastee ghoaill fo scaa dty skian.

Agh rish resoon t’ad baghtal loayrt,
’S coraa feer ooasle t’ad dy choyrt,
“Laue Yee” t’ad gra, (dy chooilley nane,)
“Ren shin y chroo,” myr t’ad soilshean.
HYMN CXXVIII.
HIARN ta mee credjal slane dty ghoan,
As farkiaght ort ayns shoh;
Fieau son dty ghialdyn derrey yioym
My niart jean ass-y-noa.
’Syn eill annoon my oddym ceau
Foast laghyn gys dty ghloyr; [132]
Yeesey, cum seose yn corp t’er-creau,
Lesh troshid roih dty phooar.
My oddys lheid yn peccagh faase
E vea ordrail dy kiart;
Cur dou (uss hrog nyn Jiarn veih baase,)
Towse ymmyrchagh dy niart.
O jean sparail mee derrey ta
Aym reesht dty ghraih breeoil;
Credjue yn cree aym er chaghlaa,
As m’annym slane gerjoil.
Dty uill dy voddym soilshagh magh,
Ta veih dagh peccah niee;
Booiagh ceau laghyn seaghnagh,
My veagh eh aigney Yee.
Shicker t’ou abyl my hauail,
Soilshee oo hene my Yee;
Jean ceau magh mac yn inney-veyl,
As tar as gow my chree.
HYMN CXXIX.
AYR, ta my annym jeeaghyn hood,
Ort ta my varrant lhie;
Credjal dy shicker dy nee void
Ta cheet, dagh nhee ta mie.
Myghin as grayse dty ghiootyn hene,
As creenagh neesht, as pooar;
Fegooish yn spyrryd ayd ta shi’yn
Cretooryn treih dy liooar.
[133]
Cha vod mayd goaill un smooinaght mie,
Ny fockle mie y loayrt,
Fegooish dty spyrryd hene, er graih
Nyn Jiarn, yn bannaght choyrt.
As she e uill ny lomarcan,
Ta er nyn son gansoor;
T’er gheddyn cooney er my hon,
’S dagh annym er yn ooir.
T’ou gobbragh’ ayns ny creeaghyn ain,
Dagh mie t’ou deayrtey sheese:
Er son y slane jean cooney lhien,
Dy chur dhyt moylley’s booise.
T’eh cur dooin pooar, hood hene dy ghuee,
Trooid Yeesey ta goaill rooin;
Aynsyn ta ain dy chooilley nhee,
As Jee ta ooilley dooin.
HYMN CXXX.
O uss, ren er yn chrosh paartail,
Kionnaght pardoon dooin lesh dty vaase
Nagh vel oo abyl nyn sauail,
As lhieeney shin lesh pooar dty ghrayse;
Yn bannaght choyrt da dagh unnane,
Lhieeney as casherickey yn slane?
She er dty oural foalley hene,
Er son dagh bannaght ta nyn dreisht:
Son she dty chrosh ta troggal shin,
Gys slane saualtys girree seose; [134]
Ayns shoh jeant firrinagh ayns graih,
As eisht meeteil kiongoyrt rish dt’oaie.
HYMN CXXXI.
NEE’M cur clashtyn, cre nee’n Chiarn
My-chione my annym loayrt!
Nagh vel gialdyn ayd er-mayrn,
Dasyn ta farkiaght ort?
Nish dy pooaral shoilshee [sic] e [sic]
Gys my annym loayrt yn goo;
Voids nagh jean-ym arragh chea,
Ny brasnagh oo ny smoo.
Kys ta mee er hassoo magh,
Noi eam dty spyrryd mie?
Lane dy roon mee-viallagh,
Er stampey er dty ghraih?
Mooghey neesht yn soilshey ayd;
Brishey void, as dy aagail,
As da grayse cha jinnin raad,
Va nastee dy hauail.
Ga lurg dou v’er yannoo wheesh,
Dy eiyrt oo voym er-sooyl;
Foast t’ou farkiaght ayns erreeish,
Neu-wooiagh dy chur cooyl:
Foast cha vel mee faagit mooie;
Agh t’ou faggys dy livrey,
Gra rhym cheet, myr ren oo roie,
Dy voddym geddyn bea.
O Eayn meen, as graihagh Yee!
Aynyds cha vel jymmoose; [135]
Agh yn ghrayse ayd dy nastee,
Ta er ny hebbal dooys:
Ta dty roighyn skeaylt dy lhean
Dy ghoaill peccee fey-ny-laa:
Uss hur baase son dagh unnane,
Bailt ooilley dy hyndaa.
Ta mish goaill oo ec dty ghoo,
Credjal ny t’ou dy ghra:
Keayney my loghtyn, scarrey roo,
Ga anmagh, hood chyndaa:
Hoods son sauchys ta mee chea,
Ooilley-niartal ta dty phooar;
Dt’uill ta loayrt sheer ass my lieh,
Ta er my hon gansoor.
HYMN CXXXII.
HIARN, ta mee credjal dy vel fea,
Da’n pobble ayd er-mayrn,
As adsyn ta goaill soylley jeh,
T’ad graihagh ort my Hiarn.
Fea, raad ta’n aigney ooilley soit
Dy firrinagh er Jee:
Peccah, as aggle tochagh, stroit
Ec graih, t’er lhieeney’n cree.
O nish dy vogh’n yn bannaght mooar,
Trooid credjue cosney eh!
Haualtagh soilshee nish dty phooar,
Veih peccah cur dou fea!
[136]
Gow ass my chree yn chreoghys shoh,
Mee-chredjue jean y stroie;
Cur dooys yn fea dy chredjue bio,
Yn doonaght jeh dty ghraih.
Soilshee dty ennym da my chree,
Dty ghooghys neesht cur dou!
Cha vel mee laccal veg agh Jee,
Veg ayns y theihll ny niau.
Haualtagh tar, ec my choraa,
Dy siyragh neose veih niau!
Nagh fuirree voym my vaynrys bra,
As uss hug toshiaght dou!
Tar neose dy leah, Hrinaid ghloyroil,
Dy chummal ayns my chree,
As glen er-sooyl dagh nhee peccoil,
As lhieen mee slane lesh Jee.
HYMN CXXXIII.
DTY uill ren roie dy heyrey mee,
Dy bragh ta cur dou aashl
Te ooilley’n gherjagh t’ec my chree,
Son aym hur Yeesey baase.
Yeesey hur baase, my Hiarn ghraysoil,
Farrane dy niee mee glen,
Jean mish y ghlenney lesh dty uill,
As freill mee still myr shen.
My nee oo mish y ghlenney Hiarn,
Beem lhiat as bee’oo my Yee:
Niee eisht my chassyn, as dagh ayrn,
My laueyn, kione, as cree.
[137]
Slane bree dty vaase cur gys my chree,
Er derrey hee’m dty oaie;
Treishteil ayns soylley coayl e vree,
As m’annym lane dy ghraih.
HYMN CXXXIV.
O Ghraih jean soilshagh oo hene dooys;
Jean niartagh’ nish my annym moal,
Paa, son cha jeanym mie dt’egooish,
Cur soilshey dou son ta mee doal:
My chree lesh trimshey nish ta lane,
My annym ching, O jean mee slaane.
My ghrian, m’endeilagh, as my lhaynt;
O tar as bee dou feer ghraysoil:
My hreisht, my varrant, as my niart,
As t’ou my attey smoo gloyroil:
My ghraih as neesht my chronney t’ou,
Eer billey’n vea as maynrys niau!
She uss yn verchys follit shen,
Va choud gyn-yss da dagh unnane,
Creest kinjagh eill ayns cree ta glen,
Yn ennym scrieut er y chlagh vane;
Yn vioys shen ta cheet veih Jee,
As niau kion-fenish nish ’sy chree.
HYMN CXXXV.
USS vochilley Israel Yee,
Lane boggey as taitnys oo dou,
Son ainjys ny sniessey rhyt, guee,
Son s’booiagh vein fuirraght raad t’ou; [138]
(Ve maroo ’syn aber messoil,
Ta biallagh da dty choraa,)
Ayns dt’oghrish, fassagh’ sheeoil,
As coodit veih’n chiass ayns y laa.
Jeeagh dou yn boayl maynrey my Yee,
Yn raad ta dty phobble goaill aash,
Ny nooghyn ayns eunys dy hee,
Goaill boggey ayns Yeesey hur baase:
O soilshee nish dooys, dty harvaant,
Cre hur oo son aym ayns yn eill:
As ’lesh mee gys Calvary mayrt,
Dy hurranse, as lurg shen dy reill.
Ayns shen dy ve marish ny eayin,
My Vochilley dooie cur dou kied,
As jean mee y assagh’ dy keayin,
Dy kinjagh rish fastee yn chreg:
Gys shen ta mee shirrey dy chion,
Dy voddym ve follit ayns Jee:
’Sy lhiattee va tholl’t er my hon,
As cummit dy bragh ayns dty chree.
HYMN CXXXVI.
MY Yee! t’aym fys dy vel mee lhiat,
Nee’m kinjagh cummal shoh,
Derrey ayns Jee veem ooilley cail’t,
As slane jeant ass-y-noa.
Cre’n tra, Haualtagh, hig yn oor?
Ne’oo soiagh’ ayns my chree,
Spyrryd dy vioys as dy phooar,
Dy reamys, as dy hee.
[139]
Yeesey, dty gthraih nagh jean failleil,
Jean skeayley ayns my chree!
’S cha jeanym oo ny smoo hreigeil,
Cha shickyr shen ayns Jee.
Dty ghraih ta coyrt yn varriaght,
Cur niart dagh peccah fo;
Yn peccah shen t’erskyn my smaght,
As croo mee ass-y-noa.
Hiarn, lhig da aile breeoil dy ghraih,
Foadey ayns m’annym nish:
Yn dross dy yeearree olk y stroie,
As lheie ny sleityn sheese.
O nish dy duittagh eh veih niau,
’S my pheccaghyn y stroie!
Tar Spyrryd Noo, cur clashtyn dou,
Tar neose ayns aile dy ghraih.
Gow trooid my annym, ghraih yn Chiarn,
Lesh soilshey ayns my chree;
As skeaul dty vioys trooid dagh ayrn,
As jean uss casherick mee.
Peccah as trimshey hig gyn bree,
Tra veem goit stiagh gys fea:
Neem’s ynrick bio dy voylley Jee,
Jee dy bragh bannit eh.
Cha jeanym dobbran ny smoo,
Tra veem jeant glen trooid grayse:
Agh jeean dy ghloyraghey m’er-croo,
Still fakin gloyr e vaaish.
[140]
HYMN CXXXVII.

Dy voddin crommey injil sheese;

My annym’ lhie ec cassyn Chreest.
Son ayd ta m’annym nish imneagh:
Haualtagh my t’ou hene my Yee.
Cur dooys dty aigney imlee feagh,
As cur dty yalloo er my chree.
Veih’n whing dy pheccah t’orrym lhie,
Hiarn, cur slane reamys da my chree,
Gyn fea gys veem slane glen cheu-sthie,
Derrey veem caillit slane ayns Jee.
S’booagh [sic: S’booiagh] bein geiyrt ort, Yee ghraysoil,
Goll trooid dagh seaghyn as angaish;
Cur lesh yn chrosh ta dhaait lesh fuill,
Eer laboragh dty ghraih ayns baase!
Baillym; agh O cur hene dou pooar!
As veih dagh peccah jean my niee:
Cur lesh er-gerrey nish yn oor,
Veem lhieent lesh boggey as lesh shee.
Tar Hiarn, lesh gerjagh ayns my chree,
Nagh lhig da’n fainagh ayd cumrail:
Agh soilshee nish oo hene my Yee;
O tar, as jean mee nish hauail.
[141]
HYMN CXXXVIII.
YN Chiarn nee’n aber aym’ordrail, [sic]
Yn bochill mie ghow’s jeem kiarail;
Cha vaag eh mish ayns feme erbee,
Lesh lane kiarail t’eh coadey mee;
’Sy laa my chesmadyn leeideil,

Dy beign failleil lesh chiass yn laa,
As er yn clieau ve faiynt as paa;
Yn boayl veagh druight, as palchey mess,
Yinnagh eh’n shaghrynagh chur lesh;
Yn boayl ta awinyn roie dy kiune,
As aber vie ry-gheddyn ayn.
Ga, coan y vaaish dy beign goll trooid,
Coodit lesh atchim, as lesh dooid,
Nhee cha vod jannoo skielley dou,
T’ou Hiarn, dy kinjagh er my heu:
Dty lorg sheeoil veagh, cur dou fea,
Veih dooyt as aggle, slane livrey.
Er cassan garroo dy beign goll,
Sy’n aasagh feayn my raad y choayl;
My hee’n agh shilley jeh e vaaish,
Yn aasagh yinnagh gennal gaase;
As messyn blaaghey siyragh dou,
As strooanyn feiyral er dagh cheu.
HYMN CXXXIX.
CREEST, er my hon ren surranse pian,
As t’eh er chionnaght mish; [142]
Dy voddin geddin ayn bea veayn,

Booise dy row hood er son dy bragh
Son slane dy vannaghtyn:
O lhig dou dty ghraih flaunyssagh,
Dy slane y ennaghtyn.
My Annym geearree jeean son Jee,
Dy chosney bannaght veih;
Myr ooilley lossey ta my chree,
Son soylley slane jeh’ ghraih.
Ny giootyn ayd, fegooish oo hene,
Cha beagh ad maynrys dou;
Dt’enish ta jannoo maynrey jeem,
As raad ta Jee ta niau.
HYMN CXL.
YN Chiarn ta rish my Hiarn er ghra,
Soie seose ayns ooashley vooar,
Gys nee dty noidjyn ooilley craa,
As crommey roish dty phooar.
Yeesey, my Hiarn, feer niartal t’ou;

Tra ta my charrey loayrt ayns niau,

Fardail da’n noid cur miolagh dou,
Son Yeesey ta my Ree.

She royds ta dooghys crommey sheese,

Gow dt’eiraght hene my Yee,
As Oh, nagh lhig da’n noid dy bragh,

Dy kinjagh ayns my chree!
Tilg sheese dagh noid ta foast er-mayrn,

Myr shen nee’m soiagh’ jeh dty reill,

Myr Shen [sic] nee’m jannoo aigney Chreest,
Myr ta ny flaunyssee:
As soilshee’m bree dty hurranse neesht,
Yn ghraih t’erskyn dagh nhee.
Yn varriaght, t’ayns Creest my Yee
Da dagh unnane nee’m ginsh:
Ta Yeesey, Chiarn erskyn dagh nhee;
Croym shiu ec ennym Creest.
[144]
Niurin as ooir nee crommey royd,
Dagh noid nee coayl nyn mree;
As baase eh hene hed naardey foyd,
Ayns ooilley t’ou uss Jee, [sic]
HYMN CXLI.
HAUALTAGH Yeesey cur ansoor,
As cur hym cooney veih dty stoyl,
As glen my chree veih graih yn ooir,
’S fow aarloo cour ayd hene, yn boayl.
O lhig da dt’enish gerjagh mee,
As gushtaghey my annym paa,
Ta geearree shoh erskyn dagh nhee
Dy veaghey ort, dagh oie as laa.
Son nhee dy vod yn seihll fordrail,
Cha jeanym aigney y chur da;
Rish seihll dy voirey as fardail,
Royd lesh dy ribbaghyn, nee’m gra,
Nee’m giyrt dy jeean da’n chassan mie,
Ayn nee oo soilshagh dou dty ghrayse:
Chamoo nee’m arragh loayrt jeh graih
Agh jeh dty ghraih’s hur son aym baase.
Nagh lhig da’n noid veih’n traa shoh magh,
Cleayney er-sooyl void hene my chree;
Son dhyts t’eh cair, eisht reill by [sic: dy] bragh,
My Hiarn, my Vainshter, as my Ree.
Berchys as ooashley, stayd ny pooar,
T’ec seihll neu-hickyr dy chur veih; [145]
Foddee shiu miolagh, neem’s gansoor,
She Creest, ta m’annym slane dy reih,
Dhyts ver-yms graih as eer dhyt hene,
Lesh taitnys glen as feer sheeoil;
Dy hoiggal uss ve soiagh’ jeem,
My Yee, cre’n stayd ta cha gerjoil!
Cha nee son berchys ta mee geam;
Agh skeayl yn ghraih ayd ayns my chree:
Cur dooys yn bannaght ta mee feme,
Eisht ver-ym seose dy chooilley nhee.
HYMN CXLII.
NAGH lhig da dooinney bio boasteil
Ass troshid, ny ass mooads e cheayl,
Chamoo ayns berchys cur treisheil,

HYMN CXLIII.
CHOUD’s nee oo traa chur dou,

HYMN CXLIV.
MY Yee, my vea, my ghraih,
She hood, she hood ta m’eam;

T’ou fondagh ayns dagh nhee

TRA t’ou my annym er ghoaill baght

CUIN, Hiarn ghraysoil cuin oddym ghra
Dy vel mee slane lhiat hene dy bra?

Cuin yioym yn eearlys jeh dty ghraih?

Dy hoiggal bree dty ghoo, my Yee.
Uss, unrick uss, baillym ve aym,
Treig-ym yn seihll as shen ny t’aym;
Cur dou oo hene, cur oo hene dou,
Roish nhee dy vel ayns shoh ny niau.
Tra yioym livrey veih’n roih dy vroid,
Nee m’annym, Yeesey getlagh hood;
Yeesey, lurg dou v’er choayl dagh nhee,
Nee m’annym lhie er dt’oghrish, Yee.
Ga dy beign treigit ec y sleih,
Cha jean oo gobbal dou dty ghraih;
T’ou booiagh goaill rish fer erbee,
Dy jean chyndaa dy ghoaill rhyts, Yee.
Ga broghe, cha jean-yms eisht dooyteil,
Shickyr cha jean oo m’y hreigeil;
Cha der oo cooyl rish boght erbee,
Ta geearree dy heet hood, my Yee.
[151]
Hiarn, ta mee ching, O cur dou couyr;
Hiarn, ta mee boght, O cur dou stoyr:
Hiarn, ta mee loobey fo dty laue,
Livrey yn peccagh boght ass gane.
Hiarn, ta mee doal, cur soilshey dou;
Ta mee annoon, bee er my heu;
Fer-coonee da’n annooinagh bee,
Gow slane my annym as my chree.
HYMN CXLIX.
CHA vel mish naaragh goaill rish Creest,
Ny dagh nhee chur fo chosh,
Ny s’leaie ny obbym gloyr e ghoo,
Ny scammylt ’chur da ’chrosh.
Yeesey, my Yee! O shone dooys eh;
Ayn ta my slane treishteil:
My annym ta cur barrant er,
Cha jean eh m’y hreigeil.
Feer ta e ghialdyn as e ghoo;
Ta echey pooar as bree
Dy reayll shen hreisht mee ayns e laue,
Gys laa mooar briwnys Yee.
Nee eh goaill rhym kionfenish ’Ayr,
As maynrys ver eh dou;
Ver eh dou ayrn ayns e reeriaght,
Yn vaynrys nagh jed mow.
HYMN CL.
TAR, Hiarn, cooin lhiam dy ghoaill boggey,
Ayns treisht dy glinn-ym dty choraa, [152]
Un laa d’akin my Yee;
Dy scuir veih ooilley my pheccah,
Loaghtey as blashtyn goo yn vea;
As gennaght fuill y chee.
Cha beeyms still ayns trimshey ceau,
Ny shirveish oo Jee nagh nione dou;
Agh bee’m bio dy hoiggal
Aash dty phobble, taitnys yn chree,
Lhiurid, lheead, diunid as yrjey,
Graih Yeesey dy hauail.
Goaill boggey nish liorish credjue,
Shassoo, as veih mullagh yn clieau
Fakin yn cheer dy ghrayse;
Awinyn dy vainney as dy vill,
As messyn Phargys ta ayn still,
Ayns palchey vooar t’ad gaase.
Cheer dy arroo, as feeyn, as ooil,
Lurg aigney Yee goit dy foayroil,
Lesh dagh bannaght bannit;
Ayns shen ta’n Chiarn nyn ynrickys,
Te freayll e chloan ayns shee as grayse,
As aash dy bragh tannaght.
O dy voddin ec keayrt goll seose,
Gyn furraght er cheu shoh jeh’n vaase,
Agh nish dy ghoaill yn cheer;
Jerrey chur er my vleeantyn nish,
Peccagh, aggle, dooyt, as angaish,
As faasagh follym feayr.
[153]
Nish, O my Yeesey gow mee stiagh,
Ceau magh dty noid yn peccah broghe,
Yn aigney heiltagh ftroie;
Toilchinys dty vaase cur dou myr gioot,
As, O maroo ta casherick,
Cur dou cronney dty graih. [sic]
HYMN CLI.
DHYTS ver-yms ghraih, [sic] my niart, my hoor,
Dhyts ver-yms graih, my voggey’s crown,
Dhyts ver-yms graih, lesh my slane pooar,
Ayns dagh obbyr, as eer dhyt hene;
Dhyts ver-yms graih, derrey nee’n flame
Dy ghraih lhieeney my annym lane.
Kys va mee choud gyn tushtey jeed
Uss s’graihagh mastey cloan deiney?
Ah! kys nagh daink mee ny s’leaie hood
Oo hene nyn aash ayns dagh trimshey,
She trimshey’s nearey t’er my chree
Dy duirree mee choud void, my Yee.
Er shaghyryn jeh m’aigney hene,
Shirrey son ayd, ny-yeih goll roym;
My smooinaghtyn va skeaylt dy lhean,
Graih hug mee da dty chretooryn:
Agh my ta mee ny smoo fakin,
She trooid dty hoilshey te, O hiam.
Booise dy row hoods ta er dy rieau,
Dy vel dty hoilshey er jeet hym; [154]
Booise dy row hood ta er chur mow
My noid, ’ser slaanagh my annym:
Booise dy row hood son dty choraa,
Ta gra rish m’annym goaill boggey.
Cum seose mee ayns y traa dy ghooyt,
Nagh lhig dou goll er-shaghyryn;
My chesmad niartee dy heet hood,
Kinjagh dy ghoaill y raad dy chion;
M’annym, my eill, O Hiarn, my vian,
Jean magh lesh soilshey flaunyss hene.
Cur jeir gerjoil dy my hooillyn,
Cur Yeearree jeean nish ayns my chree
Cur da m’annym, lesh aggle meen,
Yn ghraih shen t’ec ny flaunyssee,
Dy vod my ninrt as my slane pooar
Ve kinjagh soilshagh magh dty ghloyr.
Dhyts ver-yms graih, my voggcy’s crown
Dhyts ver-yms graih, my Hiarn my Yee;
Dhyts ver-yms graih fo baggyrtyn,
Goyms boggey mooar fo lorg my Ree,
Ga failys m’eill ayns shoh lurg traa,
Dhyts ver-yms graih er son dy bra.
HYMN CLII.
Son Sheshaght Ny Nooghyn.
ER jeet dy cheilley ass-y-noa,
Dy hebbal bioose da’n Chiarn;
Cre’n yindys eh dy vel shin bio,
Fud whilleen gaue er-mayrn?
[155]
Da’n Jee ta choud er nyn sparail
Lhig dooin cur gloyr as booise:
Ta coadey, as goaill j’in kiarail,
Ayns mean dangeyryn wheesh.
Dangeyryn ta ass shilley sooill,
’S shen ver mayd tastey da,
Yn chramp ’sy dorraghys ta shooyl,
As stroider y vun-laa.
O trog-jee nyn goraaghyn seose,
As lhig dooin voylley eh;
T’er eam shin veih yn dorraghys,
Dy hooyl ayns raad y vea.
Lhig dooin coyrt moylley son e ghrayse,
Erskyn dy chooilley nhee;
Dy daink nyn Jiarn’s dy hur eh’n baase,
Dy choardail shin rish Jee.
O lhig dooin lesh un aigney prayll.
Er son eh Spyrryd mie;
Dy jean eh shin ’sy raad leeideil,
Gys nee mayd roshtyn thie.
Ayns shen cha bee paartail dy bragh;
Agh marish flaunyssee,
Goaill jeh ny messyn flaunyssagh
Dy bragh, as moylley Jee.
HYMN CLIII.
ROISH stoyl yn ooilley-niartal Jee.
Croym-jee chloan deiney imlee sheese, [156]
Goaill rish dy ve nyn Jiarn, as Ree;
Ren croo, as oddys stroie shin reesht.
Eh ren nyn groo, hug dooin nyn mioys,
Marish dagh olt, as gioot ta mie;
Myr kirree-cailjey skeayl shin voish:
Agh ren eh reesht ’chur lesh shin thie.
Lesh kiaull dy voylley, as toyrt-booise,
Stiagh er dty ghiattyn nee mayd goll,
As jeih thousaneyn chengey, vees
Lhieenney [sic] ny cooyrtyn ayd lesh kiaull.
Dy ghraih ta farraghtyn dy bra,
Ynrick, as shicker ta dty ghoo,
(Myr creg nagh vod ve er ny chraa:)
Tra nagh bee traa, ayns shoh ny smoo.
HYMN CLIV.
TAR shiuish ta er jyndaa
Er Sion heose nyn gree,
Ayns Creest, lhig dooin dagh traa
Meeteil dy voylley Jee;
Ayns Creest lhig dooin nyn mea leeideil,
Gys nee mayd mysh e stoyll meeteil.
Ny sniessey ta shin cheet
Gys cheer ny flaunyssee:
Yn balley casherick,
Boayl fea da troailtee skee:
Jerusalem ta er-nyn-skyn,
Yn boayl dy ghraih, nee farraghtyn.
[157]
Shi’yn ta nyn gloan da Jee,
Yn seihll slane feoh cur da,
Gys ynnyd ard-veaghee,
Lesh kiaull gherjoil chyndaa;
Still goaill jurnaa lesh niart as bree,
As boggey flaunyss ayns nyn gree.
Trooid credjue bio ayns Creest,
T’ain kinjagh boggey ’s aash:
Livreit veih peccah neesht,
Veih niurin, pian as baase,
Ta shin nish goll gys thie nyn Ayr
Dy veeteil Yeesey Creest nyn Mraar.
Nyn Mraar, as Saualtagh,
T’eh dooin dy chooilley nhee;
As ad ta firrinagh,
Hee eh lesh gerjagh cree:
Hee mayd eh marish ooilley chloan,
Ayns niau bee slane nyn droailt ec kione.
HYMN CLV.
TAR shiu lhig dooin lesh un chree
Arrane-moyllee ghoaill gys Jee;
Lhig dagh annym nish goaill ayrn,
Ayns cur gloyr da Creest nyn Jiarn!
Laueyn, creeghyn as coraa
’Hroggal dy chur moylley da:
Boggey flaunyss, yiow mayd veih,
Cuirraghyn gerjoil dy ghraih.
Lhig dooin lesh un aigney streeu:
Jeh’n un spyrryd bannit giu; [158]
Jeean, myr va ny credjuee
Hur yn baase er graih Mac Yee,
Eait, dy veaghey myr ad shen,
Graihagh, maynrey as cha glen;
Credjue bio ayns Yeesey, ta
Sauail shin, veih’n chorree vra.
Creest ta’n Un er jin as jea:
Lhig dooin kinjagh’ voylley eh:
Cour ain ooilley ayns dagh saase
Creest ta kinjagh lane dy ghrayse:
Shi’yn son Creest ta shassoo jiu,
Soilshey ayns cheer ta fo scado;
Goaill rish Creest er son nyn Ree,
Shi’yn ta feanishyn son Jee.
Feanishyn jeh surranse Chreest,
Er nyn grossey marish neesht:
Pooar, y vaaish ta echey brisht,
Liorish er nyn mioghey nish:
Ayns ard ooashley t’eh er hoie;
Huggey ta nn greeghyn roie;
Gloyr dy bragh mygeayrt y mysh;
Shi’yn ayns graih freayll sheshaght rish.
HYMN CLVI.
MY Vraaraghyn deyr,
Ta troilt gys bea veayn;
Dy nastee jeant seyr,
Trooid surranseyn Eayn:
Son ain cha row toilliu,
Ny creenaght, ny niart; [159]
Agh geddyn ad ooilley
Trooid Creest ghow nyn baart.
Cha nee lane sleih mooar
Ta soiagh jeh’n cheb,
Ta feallagh ayns pooar
Jeh’n raad coontey beg:
Cha jean feallagh lajer
Goaill rish dy ve faase;
Ny shirrey ayns padjer,
Saualtys trooid grayse.
As myr shen ta Jee
Yn fooilliagh er reih,
Er hoilshagh e hee
Eer dooin va cha treih;
Tra va’n vooinjer chreeney
Er nobbal dy heet,
Va dreighyn dy gheiney
’Syn ynnyd oc eit.
Shi’yn boghtyn dy leih,
Va beg coontit j’in;
Agh Creest t’er nyn reih,
’S er ghoaill shin da hene
Son gloyr as son maynrys
T’eh jannoo shin cooie;
Cur lesh hooin saualtys,
As goaill rooin dy dooie.
T’eh Ree as Phadeyr,
As Saggyrt dooin neesht; [160]
Lhig dooin myr te cair
Cur moylley da Creest;
Mee-hushtee t’eh gynsagh,
Nyn lheid t’eh dy reih;
Dy voddagh ad soilshagh,
Eer berchys e ghraih.
Yn troailtagh ta shooyl
Yn cassan gys bea;
Cha Baghtal da’n thooill,
Cha lhiass da goll jeh;
Yn credjueagh s’oney,
Yow ennaght jeh Jee;
Jeh ghraih yiow eh soylley
Myr awin ayns e chree.
HYMN CLVII.
GLOYR gys nyn Jiarn t’er chionnaght shin,
T’er sneimmey shin ayns grayse,
As gra rooin mennick cur meeteil,
Dy vod mayd aynsyn gaase.
Dagh braar t’eh gra rooin choyrlaghey,
Yn goo y ynsagh daue,
Dy vod yn slane ayns grayse y Chiarn
Goll cooidjagh laue ry laue.
Yn gerjagh shen t’eh cur da ’nane,
Lheid baillin ’ennaght veih,
As nee e ’ghrayse y chur da’n claane,
Ayns strooanyn dy ghraih.
Eer nish ta’n smooinaght ain unnane,
Ta ain yn un chiarail, [161]
Ayns shirveish Yeesey ta dagh cree
Ta ayns shoh er choardail.
She boggey ’nane ta boggey’n claane,
As gennaght yn un shee,
Yn shee shen ta gyn-yss da’n seihll,
Yn boggey t’ain ayns Jee:
As my ta’n sheshaght t’ain ayns shoh
Rish Yeesey cha gerjoil,
Lane s’maynrey vees mayd lurg y vaase,
Tra hee mayd eh gloyroil.
HYMN CLVIII.
TAR uss Hiarn, ayns gloyr t’ou reill;
Keayrt dy ghooiney ayns yn eill:
Lhig dooin fakin soilshey dt’oaie;
Tar gys cooney boghtyn treih;
Ta shin jerkal rhyt dy heet,
Gys ny cuirraghyn t’ou eait:
Kiartee cour ayd hene dagh cree,
Skeayl roin board, dy vod mayd gee.
Aynyds Yeesey, er veeteil;
Son dty yialdyn ta shin prayll;
Nish ayns dt’aalid jean soilshean,
Soilshee oo hene ayns y vean:
Bannee shin lesh glennid cree;
Lane jeh’n spyrryd lane dy hee;
Jean dty yialdyn ’yannoo mie;
Cur dooin cuirraghyn dy ghraih.
Lhig dooin bishaghey as gaase
Ayns dagh gioot as mess dy ghrayse: [162]
Credjue, graih, as boggey lesh,
Sheeltys, as dagh nhee ta jesh;
Cur dooin dt’aigney imlee hene,
Surranse-foddey, dooie as meein;
Inj’lid firrinagh ayns cree:
Lane dy vieys, lane jeh Jee.
Aynyds, lhig dooin aarloo’ve! [sic]
Dy chur lesh shin seose gys bea;
Marish yn chioaltane t’ou reih,
Feeu dy hassoo roish dy oaie;
Eie shin gys yn vaynrys beayn,
Thie gys shibbyr banshey’n ’Eayn;
Lhig dooin er dty oghrish lhie,
Geddyn cronney beayn dy ghraih!
HYMN CLIX.
LHIG dooin goll gys yn aer,
My vraaraghyn deyr,
As booise chur da Jee son y laa;
Lhig dooin laue-ry-laue
Goll siyragh gys niau:
Lesh singal gys sion chyndaa.
Yn stoyr ain ta heose,
Ny n’ghraih as nyn dreisht,
Nyn girp fud yn seihll noon as noal:
Cloan chionnit yn’ Eayn,
Dy chosney bea veayn,
Lesh singal gys paradise goll.
Lesh taitnys cur booise,
Son graih Yeesey Creest, [163]
Nyn mioys cur dooin as dagh nhee;
Son giootyn cha feoilt,
Te cairagh dooin coyrt
Gloyr, moylley, as ooashley, da Jee.
Son dty ghloyr ta shin crooit,
Dy ghoaill ayrn ayns dagh gioot,
Chammah dooghysagh as spyrrydoil:
Agh crooit ass-y-noa,
Mayrt hene dy ve bio,
Ayns shoh, as ’syn eiraght gloyroil.
HYMN CLX.
FEYSHT shin O Yee; as ronsee grunt
Dagh cree ta foast peccoil;
As my yiow aynin loght erbee,
O niee yn slane er-sooyl.
My ta shin goll er-shaghryn,
Ny faag gyn cooney shin;
Agh lieed uss reesht nyn gesmadyn
Thie gys yn shee ayd hene.
Cooin lhien dy chooney lesh nyn mraar,
Ayns seaghyn goaill rish ayrn;
Dy choyrlagh’ eh, dy vod eh ve
Ny harvaant dooie dhyts, Hiarn.
Jean cur dty vannaght er nyn goyrle,
As cur dooin yeearree jeean;
Niartee nyn gredjue as nyn dreisht,
As lhieen shin lesh graih veayn.
[164]
Lhig dooin gaase seose hoods ta nyn gione,
Jean’ chroo shin ass-y-noa;
Dy vod mayd nish’ ve seyrit liort,
As glen gyn cron ayns shoh.
Eisht tra ta’n obbyr niartal jeant,
As slane nyn beccah maiht;
Gow shin gys niau as cur dooin ayrn,
Marish dty vooinjer reiht.
HYMN CLXI.
Son yn Aarkey.
OBBYR y Chiarn ’sy diunid heese,
As pooar e laue ghloroil;
Jeh shoh nee deiney dunnal ginsh,
Ta er yn aarkey goll.
Yn gheay t’eh sarey, ta cur er
Yn keayn myr tooryn gaase;
Deiney ta troggit seose ’syn aer,
’S goll sheese ayns lhiaghtyn baaish.
Ny sleityn ushtey t’ad goll noi,
Agh sheese reesht er ny cheau;
Myr dooinney meshtal er ny heiy,
Leaystey veih cheu dy cheu.
Ny tonnyn freaney’s scoltey moo,
Feer agglagh t’ad jeh gaue:
Yn purt ta jeeaghin foddey voue,
Agh baase feer faggys daue.
Gys Jee t’ad geam ta cur-my-ner, [165]
As clashtyn nyn goraa;
T’eh cur magh sarey trooid yn aer,
’S ta ooilley kiune chyndaa.
Aggle shiolteyryn eisht t’er-sooyl,
Yn sterrym ta ec shee:
Yn purt ta cronnal roish nyn sooyll,
Ayn lhig daue moylley Jee.
Jee ta livrey ad ass dagh gaue;
Lhig deiney jeh goaill baght,
Dy vel yn faarkey fo e laue,
As geayghyn fo e smaght.
O eisht dy jinnagh deiney booise
Da’n Chiarn’s gyn ve nyn daaue,
Agh jeh e vieys kinjagh ginsh,
E ghraih gys cloan sheelnaue!
HYMN CLXII.
Son y Ree.
DY bannee Jee nyn Ree:
Cur sauchys da, as shee
Traa dy ghangeyr:
Uss lhig da gennaghtyn,
Myr shoh dy hannaghtyn;
Cur neose dty vannaghtyn
Flaunyssagh er.
Ayns annym, corp, as cooid,
Freill eh veih pooar yn noid;
Jean eh gerjoil:
Freill eh veih irree magh [166]
Yn theay mee-viallagh;
Cairys as craueeaght,
Niartagh’e stoyl.
Yee vie jean soiagh’ jeh
Lheid as ta ass e lieh,
Geam son ansoor:
Bannit lesh aggle Yee,
Lesh credjue, graih, as shee,
Gys Nagh bee lheid y ree,
Er ooilley’n ooir.
Giall neesht myr-geeddyn da,
Son foddey liauyr dy hraa,
Dy ve nyn gione:
Fo-syn lhig agglish Chreest,
Bishagh’ as blaaghey neeaht;
Jean eh fendeilagh jeeish,
Ayr da dty chloan.
Nish ayns e hraa, cur magh
Dty ghoo lesh barriaght,
Barriaght vooar:
Aigh vie as raad cur da,
Roish lhig da peccee craa:
Gys nee’n slane ream chyndaa,
Goaill-rish dty phooar.
As tra ne’oo fakin mie,
D’eam nyn ard reiltagh thie;
O cur eh lesh
Gys yn kion-fenish ayd,
Cur da ayns yrjey stayd [167]
Crown lesh ny s’lee rollage,
Ec dty laue yesh.
HYMN CLXIII.
Son yn Ashoon.
HIARN bee’n Ashoon shoh giarit jeh
Gys nearey son dy bra?
As vel dty chorree gaase cha cheh,
Nagh jean oo hooin chyndaa?
Dy vriwnyssyn ta gagglagh’ shin,
Cur er nyn niart failleil;
Myr deiney meshtyllagh lesh feeyn,
Er-creau lesh mee-hreishteil.
Son ta’n Ashoon shoh gleashaght nish,
Fo baggyrt pooar dty laue:
O jean sauail eh roish t’eh brisht,
As lhig da nish goaill raaue.
Trog seose dty chullee er nyn son,
Dy choadey shin tar royd;
Sheeyn magh dty shleiy as freill dty chloan,
’S gys nearey cur y noid.
O jean ny pooar’ghyn ain ordrail,
Bee Jee dy chemmyrk daue;
Eisht yiow nyn noidyn e fardail,
Dy hassoo noi dty laue.
Eisht bee yn phooar ayd er ny insh,
T’er choadey shin veih baase: [168]
Jee ta cur treanee niartal sheese,
As troggal deiney faase.
HYMN CLXIV.
Son Preachoor.
YEESEY dy ynrickys, as pooar,
Cur magh dty haghter yn Preachoor,
Cum seose e laueyn Hiarn, as chiow
E chree lesh aile breeoil veih niau.
Bee beeal, as creenaght da dty loayrt,
Bwoaill lesh dty ghoo breeoil myr oayrd,
As jean ny creeghyn creoi y vroo;
Ynsee da taarnagh magh dty ghoo:
Agh daue ta son y Chiarn waiteil,
Cur da yn goo dy ghrayse ’phreacheil,
Cur er ny creeghyn oc dy lheie,
Dy veelagh lesh yn aile dy ghraih.
Dy booisal lhig dy chooilley chree,
Goaill-rish myr chaghter huc veih Jee;
Yn goo t’eh loayrt, cur eh lesh bree,
Trooid pooar dty spyrryd, gys dagh cree.
HYMN CLXV.
Son y Doonaght.
SHE jiu, ta’n Chiarn cheet gys e chloan,
As jeanyms lhiastey furriaght voue,
Nagh jem dy eaishtagh rish e ghoan?
Ta Yeesey geam ta room ayn dou, [169]
Hem gys yn boayl ta nooghyn prayll,
Son foddee Jee, cur dou meeteil.
Ta Yeesey jiu beaghey ’hioaltane,
Dagh creestee geddyn Creest, nyn ree
E heshaght-caggee goaill arrane,
As ginsh ny femeyn oc, da Jee:
Hem maroo neesht, son ta mee eait,
Ta Yeesey ghraihagh gra rhym cheet.
S’foddey ren Anna chrauee streeu!
As shirrey’n Chiarn, son kiare-feed blein:
Dagh oie as laa ’sy chiamble fieau,
As gys yn Chiarn lesh jeeanid geam:
Gyn thie yn phadjer y hreigeil,
Gys ren ee’n Chiarn ayns shen veeteil.
Haualtagh vannit, giall dou pooar,
Marish dty nooghyn eisht nee’m guee:
Dy booiagh farkiaght son yn oor,
Tra vees oo soilshit ayns my chree;
As ayns dty yiattyn ceau my hraa,
Fieau son dty chenjallys dagh laa.
