II.	Cha bee kerraghey ayns Cooish Chimmeeys gyn Briwnys dy chyndid
liorish Bing.
As gyn Screeuyn-fo-loo jeant rolaue.
Nee Quaiylyn Pooar y ’reayll dy phryssooney adsyn ta Mee-arrymagh daue.
Sy nah ynnyd, As lhig da ve ny sodjey focklit magh, oardit, as jeant ny leigh
liorish y Torritee roie-raait, Nagh bee sy traa ry-heet Pooar ny Torritee ec
Quaiyl, Briw, ny briw beg erbee syn Ellan shoh dy chur Oolaghyn ny
Kerraghyn er Persoon ny Persoonyn erbee syn Ellan roie-raait, son ny er
Coontey jeh Cooish chimmeeys erbee, derrey vees eh, ee, ny ad deyrit liorish
Briwnys ny Toyrtys-fysseree veih kiare, shey, ny ny smoo, dy Gheiney, myr
vees femoil da’n Chooish, cordail rish Leigh ennagh Scruit t’ayns bree syn
Ellan roie-raait, ny dy chur ayns pryssoon Persoon ny Persoonyn erbee dy
meechairagh roish my bee Plaiynt chiart jeant as recortyssit, as Screeuyn-fo-
loo jeant bentyn da’n Firrinys j’ee.
(1)	Er conaant nagh bee yn Leigh shoh lhaiht ny toiggit dy lhiettal persoon
erbee t’er surranse aggair veih’n Vondeish jeh Pleadeil noi’n chassid
cordail rish shenn-chliaghtey.
(2)	dys (4) ..
(5)	As dy bee ec Quaiylyn Briwnys as Briwnyn Beggey choud’s t’ad
cooilleeney curmyn nyn oikyn y Phooar t’ayn nish as vees tannaghtyn dy
chur ayns pryssoon ny dy chur ooley er Persoon ny Persoonyn erbee, rere
Dooghys y loght, son Ymmyrkey mee-arrymagh, jannoo faghid orroo ny
goltooaney ad, ny fer erbee jeu, choud’s t’ad jannoo nyn Gurrym.1
