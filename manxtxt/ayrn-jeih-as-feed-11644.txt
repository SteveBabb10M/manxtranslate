The Vampire Murders

Ayrn Jeih as Feed

Dunveryssyn yn Tooder-Folley - The Vampire Murders    Skeeal straneagh liorish Brian Stowell: Ayrn 30  A serial story by Brian Stowell: Part 30   V'eh goll dy vriaght jee reesht row ee laccal soie sheese, agh va shen fardailagh. 
 Dreggyr eh dy moandagh: 'Uh..hooar ad dy row Mainshtyr Mylvoirrey marroo ayns lhiabbee 
sy thie-goaldee..Uh..er..Cha nel scrutaght eear-vaase jeant foast... as..uh..shen 
ooilley foddym gra ec y traa t'ayn, Venainshtyr..'  Jarrood y daa howl sy wannal echey 
son y traa v'ayn. Cha row Amy goll dy vriaght c'red va Askell jannoo ayns thie-goaldee ayns Doolish 
as eisht çheet dy ve marroo.  Begin jee tannaghtyn kiune as mooaralagh sy scammylt 
ommidjagh shoh.  V'ee guee dy jinnagh ny meoiryn-shee shoh gimmeeaght magh ass as 
veagh ee abyl dy screeaghey magh lesh ferg noi'n ellan bolvaneagh shoh. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- V'eh goll dy vriaght jee..(vay-GULL-the-VRY-akht-jee) - He was going to ask(of) her Use 'briaght' for asking questions and 'shirrey er' for asking someone to do something.  ..row ee laccal soie sheese..(ROW-ee-LALL-SA-ee-SHEESS) - ..if she wanted to   sit 
down..('..was she wanting sitting down..') Manx doesn't have 'if' here.  ..agh va shen fardailagh. (akh-va-SHEN-far-DAAL-akh) -..but that was futile. ..hooar ad dy row Mainshtyr Mylvoirrey marroo..(HOOR-ad-the-ROW-MINE-shter-  mul-VURR-a-MARR-oo) 
- ..they found that Mr Mylvoirrey was dead.. Cha nel scrutaght eear-vaase jeant..(ha-NELL-SKROOT-akht-EER-VAASS-jinnt) -   A post 
mortem examination hasn't been done..('An examination after death   is not done..') ..shen ooilley foddym gra..(shen-ULL-ya-FOTH-um-GRAA) -..that's all I can say.. Jarrood y daa howl sy wannal echey..(jarr-OOD-a-DAA-HOLE-sa-WANN-al-EGG-  a) - Forget 
the two holes in his neck.. Remember that nouns stay in the singular after 'daa' and that 'daa' causes letter 
changes: 'towl' - 'hole', 'daa howl' 'two holes'.  ..c'red va Askell jannoo ayns thie-goaldee ayns Doolish as eisht çheet
dy ve marroo. (KERR-id-va-ASK-ell-JINN-oo-unnss-TY-GAWL-dee-unnss-DOO-
lish-azz-ESH-chitt-the-vay-MARR-oo) - ..what Askell was doing in a
guest house in Douglas and then ending up dead. ('..what Askell was
doing in a guest house in Douglas and then coming to be dead.') Begin jee tannaghtyn kiune as mooaralagh..(BYD'N-jee-TANN-akht-un-K'YOON-  azz-MOOR-al-akh) 
- She had to stay calm and aloof... ..sy scammylt ommidjagh shoh. (sa-SKAMM-ilt-OMM-idj-akh-shaw) - ..In this    ridiculous 
scandal. V'ee guee dy jinnagh ny meoiryn-shee shoh gimmeeaght magh
ass..(vee-GWEE-
the-JINN-akh-na-MERR-un-SHEE-shaw-GIMM-ee-akht-MAKH-ASS) - She was
praying that these policemen would clear off out of it.. ..as veagh ee abyl dy screeaghey magh..(azz-VEE-ukh-ee-AABL-the-    SKREEKH-a-MAKH) 
-..and she would be able to scream out.. ..noi'n ellan bolvaneagh shoh. (nine-ELL-yan-bol-VAAN-akh-shaw) - ..against this   stupid 
island.
